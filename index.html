About

Tristan van Wyk
Game Design & Developer

Creative, ambitious, and inspiring. My out-the-box approach to problem solving and steadfast determination pushes me to innovate on the exceptional!
I've dipped my toes into many different fields of design and found a small home in all of them. My ambitions have taken me from programming to illustration and the desire to leave an impact with my work continues to drive its creation. I'm still developing my skill sets in order to take on even greater challenges in these creative spaces, ultimately aiming to shake up the game development space through collaborative effort.

The stories and moments that are created through their unique methods of interactivity set in stone my desire to create and share them with others. It's my hope that what I create will someday have a meaningful impact on the lives of those that interact with my work, much like others did for me.

Out-Run

Tasked to create a "lane runner" style game and armed with a very limited knowledge of 3D modeling, I chose a theme I felt would be both manageable and fit the meditative gameplay of the genre, "Outrun". The game sends you through an endless neon horison, where you will need fast reflexes and quick thinking to outmaneuvere not only the enviroment but the big bad bosses that stand in your way.

Hear Me

This experiance sets you in the shoes of a girl struggling with the anxiety of being unheard by her parents, however lost in hear own head she finds somone whe seems to be all ears. This demo makes use of a node tree and quest system that can track your decisions for use in the envoroment.
